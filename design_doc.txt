Title:
    Project Common Ground

Gameplay:
    Wander around building structures and infrastructure to support the life of various species from different climates and planets. These structures will cost either materials, money, or both.

Backstory:
    The civilizations of various planets across the galaxy have come together to build a land where anyone can live peacefully together. The council in charge of this "Project Common Ground" have chosen you (the player) to be the leader of construction.
